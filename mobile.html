Description

BillMate is a professional event finance and management tool designed to simplify complex billing calculations and track project finances with precision. It serves as a comprehensive companion for event planners and finance managers.

Main Features

  • Project Management

    Create multiple event projects, each with its own isolated billing data, guest counts, and customized services.

  • Menu Library

    Build and store reusable food and beverage menus. Add entire menu groups to your project bill with a single click.

  • Finance

    Manage income and expenses across different currencies with real-time balance tracking and transaction history.

  • Professional Receipts

    Generate clean, professional receipts for your clients that can be printed or shared directly from your device.
